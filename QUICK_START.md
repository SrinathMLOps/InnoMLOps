## 🚀 How to Test the Form

1. Go to your website: https://srinathmlops.github.io/InnoMLOps/
2. Fill out the contact form
3. Submit it
4. **IMPORTANT**: Check the inbox of **ceo@innomlopssolutions.com**
5. You'll receive a confirmation email from FormSubmit
6. Click the confirmation link in that email
7. Done! All future submissions will work automatically

### After Confirmation:
- Every form submission sends to BOTH email addresses
- You'll receive formatted emails with all form details
- Customer gets auto-confirmation (optional)

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Services dropdown not working?**
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+F5)
- Wait 2-3 minutes for GitHub Pages to rebuild
- Try in incognito mode

**Form not sending?**
- Check if you confirmed the email (first time only)
- Check spam folder
- Verify internet connection
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

**Not receiving emails?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email addresses are correct
- Wait a few minutes (can take 1-2 minutes)
- Check both email inboxes
