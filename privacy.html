Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

YourBusiness Ltd ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website or contact us.

1. Data We Collect

When you submit the enquiry form, we collect: your name, email address, phone number, company name, and message content. We also collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type) for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide the services you've requested
  • To send you relevant information about our services (only if you've consented)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data on the basis of your consent (given via the form checkbox) and our legitimate interest in responding to business enquiries.

4. Data Retention

We retain enquiry data for a maximum of 24 months, after which it is securely deleted unless a business relationship has been established.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@yourbusiness.com.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies only to ensure the website functions correctly. No third-party tracking cookies are used without your consent.

7. Contact

For privacy queries: YourBusiness Ltd, 123 Business Lane, London EC1A 1BB | privacy@yourbusiness.com
